Our Team

Aspiria Softwares Pvt. Ltd. is led by a multidisciplinary team combining entrepreneurial leadership, operational depth, and strong technical execution. Together, the team brings decades of experience across finance, operations, technology, and human capital development.

Namrata Jain

Co-Founder & Head of Finance

Namrata Jain is a highly experienced entrepreneur and business leader with a strong track record of successfully founding and operating companies. As Co-Founder and Head of Finance at Aspiria Softwares Pvt. Ltd., she provides strategic financial oversight and long-term business direction.

Dr. Naini Gupta

Co-Founder & Head of Operations

Naman Sagar

Head of Information Technology
